Masala Chai

Chai. India's most popular beverage. And monsoon just calls for chai (like you really need an excuse)! Having a cup of tea while munching on some hot pakodas is definitely everyone's ideal rainy day. We've developed five chai recipes for you, guaranteed to make you feel warm and fuzzy. Exactly the kind of feeling a good cup of tea leaves you with.

    Ingridients

  • Milk
  • Tea powder
  • cardamom
  • Ginger
  • Lemon grass
  • Water
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Sugar/Jaggery

    Instructions:-

  1. Take out the seeds from a cardamom firstly and crush it well.
  2. Crush a piece of ginger too (without skin).
  3. In a vessel, bring 1 & ½ cups of water to rolling boil.
  4. Add crushed ginger, cardamom, cinnamon and clove.
  5. Grate or sprinkle a pinch of nutmeg, 2 teaspoons of tea powder.
  6. Simmer for 3 minutes.
  7. Add ½ cup of milk and 2 teaspoons of sugar.
  8. Bring to a boil again and simmer for 4 more minutes.
  9. Filter through a metal strainer lastly and serve hot.

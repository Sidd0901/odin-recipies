Solkadh A Refreshing Coastal Delight

Solkadhi is a tantalizing coastal drink originating from the Konkan region of India. This refreshing concoction not only serves as a thirst-quencher but also boasts a unique combination of flavors that dance on your palate. With its vibrant pink hue, Solkadhi is made from a delightful blend of kokum, coconut milk, and an array of aromatic spices. This cooling beverage is not only a popular choice during scorching summers but also makes a perfect accompaniment to spicy Konkani and Malvani dishes. Let's dive into the ingredients and steps to create this invigorating Solkadhi at home.

    Ingridients:-

  • 1 cup dried kokum petals
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • A small piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Mustard seeds for tempering
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

    Instructions:-

    li>Prepare Kokum Extract: Soak dried kokum petals in 1 cup of warm water for about 1 hour. After soaking, squeeze the kokum to extract its juice. Set aside.
  1. Make Coconut Paste: In a blender, combine grated coconut, chopped green chilies, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, minced garlic, grated ginger, and curry leaves. Blend into a smooth paste using water as needed.
  2. Strain Kokum Extract: Strain the soaked kokum extract to remove any solids, obtaining a clear, dark pink liquid.
  3. Combine Kokum Extract and Coconut Paste: Mix the strained kokum extract with the coconut paste. Add 2 cups of buttermilk and salt to taste. Stir well to combine.
  4. Prepare Tempering: In a small pan, heat cooking oil. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Pour the tempering over the Solkadhi mixture.
  5. Garnish and Chill: Garnish the Solkadhi with fresh coriander leaves. Refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours to let the flavors meld.
  6. Serve Chilled: Pour the chilled Solkadhi into glasses and serve as a refreshing and flavorful drink to complement your favorite coastal dishes.
